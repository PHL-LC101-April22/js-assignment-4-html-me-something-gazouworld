Owen Sound, Ontario

I was born in Owen Sound, Ontario, Canada. Owen Sound is famous as 'The Home of Bobby Orr'.

Skunk Hollow, Nova Scotia

In a rash descision, my parents decided to move us into the woods of Nova Scotia, Canada. We built our own log cabin and lived without plumbing or electricity for several years.

Dalhousie University Theatre School

I auditioned and was accepted to the Dalhousie University Theatre School. I learned how to overunciate and how to use an inappropriate ammount of makeup.

Theatre Newfoundland and Labrador

I got my first professional acting job in Newfoundland! While I was there a guy asked me if I wanted to work in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I politly turned him down and said I was going to try my luck in film and television in Vancouver.

21 Jump Street

I was a glorified extra for a few months on the show 21 Jump Street. Then they moved me up to 'special status'extra. I'm still not sure what that means. But I hung in there long enough to get a speaking line. I can't find the episode so you'll have to trust me that I was in it.

Hedgerow Theatre Company

I had loftier ambitions than film and tv. Translated: Paul couldn't get another gig. So I remembered that offer to come work in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I took the leap and went to Hedgerow Theatre Company.

LaunchCode

I'm still staying active in theatre but I thought I'd try my hand a coding. I made the word 'coding' bold to show our intructors that I learned one of the 6,0000.00000 html tags. The word 'bold', however is html tag 'strong' for our visually impared users.
I'll let you know how my coding journey goes.
